Strongest MLS-safe phrase: do not change room dimensions, wall placement, ceiling height, window size, window placement, door placement, or camera perspective

How to get better results

  • Use original straight-on listing photos when possible. Wide-angle photos give the AI more room to distort.
  • Tell it what to change and what to leave alone in the same prompt.
  • Use words like “same exact layout,” “same exact perspective,” and “no structural changes.”
  • If it keeps stretching a room, ask for a cosmetic update only instead of a full redesign.
  • For MLS compliance, avoid prompts that imply additions, removed walls, raised ceilings, or expanded square footage unless clearly labeled elsewhere.

This tool does not give legal or MLS policy advice. It is meant to help you write safer visual prompts that stay closer to the real photo.
